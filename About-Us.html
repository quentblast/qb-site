About Us

QuentBlast is the result of an inside joke
gone too far.
The absurdity of a bowling RPG is obvious,
but the sheer insanity required to spend years
programming it into reality is more insepid.

Joe Cano is the lead musician, Quentin Holmes the
lead artist, and Alex Araujo the lead programmer.
Depending on what goes wrong while you play,
you can blame one of us (probably Alex).

Truthfully, none of us had any game development
experience going in. It took nearly three years
of programming and many, MANY revisions
 and changes, but here it is-
the first private demo at least.

Though the game began as a joke, we are looking
for serious feedback! Be scathing
 and truthful, our feelings may be hurt
(and Quentin may cry a little),
but it's better to hurt them now rather than later.

Thank you so much in advance for trying out
our game. We hope you enjoy playing it as much as
we enjoyed making it!
