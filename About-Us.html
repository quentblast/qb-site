About Us

QuentBlast is the result of an inside joke
gone too far.
The absurdity of a bowling RPG is obvious,
but the sheer insanity required to spend years
programming it into reality is more insepid.

Joe Cano is the lead musician, Quentin Holmes the
lead artist, and Alex Arajo the lead programmer.
Depending on what goes wrong while you play,
you can blame one of us (probably Alex lmao).

The game has gone through many revisions
and truthfully none of us had any game development
experience going in. It has taken three years
of work, but we're finally almost ready to
present the demo to you.

We hope you are as excited for the demo as we are!
-The CronJobs
